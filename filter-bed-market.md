<p><p>Filter bed markets are classified into two main types: natural and artificial. Natural filter beds utilize geological formations, such as sand or gravel layers, to purify water as it passes through, harnessing the natural filtration process. In contrast, artificial filter beds are man-made systems designed to mimic this process, often comprising layers of various materials to enhance filtration efficiency. Both types aim to improve water quality but employ different methods and structures to achieve effective filtration.</p></p>
