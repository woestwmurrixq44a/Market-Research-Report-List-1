<p><p>The Commercial Vehicle Fastener Market consists of two main types: threaded fasteners and non-threaded fasteners. Threaded fasteners, such as bolts and nuts, are designed to provide secure connections, allowing for easy assembly and disassembly in vehicles. Non-threaded fasteners, including rivets and clips, offer permanent solutions for joining components, enhancing structural integrity. Both types are crucial for the reliability and safety of commercial vehicles, ensuring optimal performance under various operational conditions. Their selection impacts maintenance and operational efficiency.</p></p>
